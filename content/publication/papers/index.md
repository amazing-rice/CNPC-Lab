<div class="pub-detail-container">

  <div class="pub-intro">
    地震物理模型实验室长期聚焦于复杂油气储层地震识别的基础理论与实验研究。近年在<b>方位各向异性裂缝预测、多波波场机理分析及人造岩石物理实验</b>等方向取得了突破性进展，相关成果发表于国内外地球物理领域核心期刊。
  </div>

  <div class="pub-entry">
    <div class="pub-main">
      <h3 class="pub-title-link">利用物理模拟地震数据验证基于方位各向异性的裂缝预测方法</h3>
      <p class="pub-abstract">构建了四川盆地龙马溪组页岩储层的物理模型，通过超声波采集和OVT道集处理，系统定量验证了四种方位各向异性裂缝预测方法的可靠性，为工区实际资料处理提供了误差评估基准。</p>
      <div class="pub-meta">刘恩良, 丁拼搏, 张峰, 霍守东, 李向阳</div>
      <a href="/publication/2024-cgj-shear-wave-splitting/" class="badge-doi">论文详情</a>
    </div>
    <div class="pub-figure"><img src="/publication/2024-cgj-shear-wave-splitting/featured.png"></div>
  </div>

  <div class="pub-entry">
    <div class="pub-main">
      <h3 class="pub-title-link">Comparison of Torsional and SH Waves in Isotropic and Anisotropic Media Based on Laboratory Measurements</h3>
      <p class="pub-abstract">分析了实验室内利用PZT晶片构建扭转(T)波换能器的物理特性，首次对比了各向同性与裂缝诱导各向异性介质中T波与SH波的传播规律，发现了横波分裂的微观机理差异。</p>
      <div class="pub-meta">丁拼搏, Xiang-Yang Li, Feng Zhang, Haitao Chen</div>
      <a href="/publication/2025-jge-torsional-wave/" class="badge-doi">论文详情</a>
    </div>
    <div class="pub-figure"><img src="/publication/2025-jge-torsional-wave/featured.png""></div>
  </div>

  <div class="pub-entry">
    <div class="pub-main">
      <h3 class="pub-title-link">Viability of fracture characterization using seismic attributes through seismic physical modeling</h3>
      <p class="pub-abstract">利用三维地震物理模型定量评估了常规地震属性在表征中观尺度裂缝中的局限性，创新性地提出了与裂缝密度高度相关的 ADF（主频振幅）敏感属性，提升了预测分辨率。</p>
      <div class="pub-meta">Enliang Liu, Feng Zhang, Shoudong Huo, 丁拼搏, Xiangyang Li</div>
      <a href="#" class="badge-doi">DOI / Full Text</a>
    </div>
    <div class="pub-figure"><img src="/publication/2025-jge-torsional-wave/featured.png"></div>
  </div>

  <div class="pub-entry">
    <div class="pub-main">
      <h3 class="pub-title-link">Analyzing the seismic response characteristics of P, SV, and SH waves to reservoir parameters using physical modeling</h3>
      <p class="pub-abstract">通过室内三维模拟，定量剖析了纵波(P)与多分量横波(SV/SH)对孔隙流体及泥质含量的地震响应差异，为多波地震资料的流体识别提供了物理实验依据。</p>
      <div class="pub-meta">丁拼搏, Feng Zhang, Xiangyang Li, Yibo Chai</div>
      <a href="#" class="badge-doi">DOI / View PDF</a>
    </div>
    <div class="pub-figure"><img src="/publication/2025-jge-torsional-wave/featured.png"></div>
  </div>

  <div class="pub-entry">
    <div class="pub-main">
      <h3 class="pub-title-link">横波分裂和纵波方位各向异性在油气勘探中的应用</h3>
      <p class="pub-abstract">系统回顾了多波各向异性理论的发展历程，重点阐述了野外数据品质及复杂近地表条件对裂缝预测效果的影响，指出了实验室物理模拟在算法验证中的不可替代性。</p>
      <div class="pub-meta">李向阳, 王赟, 孙鹏远, 李乐, 丁拼搏</div>
      <a href="#" class="badge-doi">DOI / CNKI</a>
    </div>
    <div class="pub-figure"><img src="/publication/2025-jge-torsional-wave/featured.png"></div>
  </div>

  <div class="pub-entry">
    <div class="pub-main">
      <h3 class="pub-title-link">Frequency-dependent velocities and attenuations in fluid-saturated rocks: Fractures aligned in isotropic medium with random pore shape</h3>
      <p class="pub-abstract">将Chapman模型扩展至包含硬币型裂缝与随机孔隙形状的各向同性基质中，有效预测了流体饱和岩石中地震波随频率变化的频散与衰减特征。</p>
      <div class="pub-meta">Lingyi He, Bangrang Di, 丁拼搏, Jianxin Wei</div>
      <a href="#" class="badge-doi">DOI / Theoretical Model</a>
    </div>
    <div class="pub-figure"><img src="/publication/2025-jge-torsional-wave/featured.png"></div>
  </div>

  <div class="pub-entry">
    <div class="pub-main">
      <h3 class="pub-title-link">Partial fluid saturation effects on bulk modulus and attenuation: the experiment and the theoretical model</h3>
      <p class="pub-abstract">通过超声波精细实验结合理论推导，研究了不同饱和度条件下岩石体积模量与地震波能量吸收的变化机制，揭示了岩石物理参数的非线性响应规律。</p>
      <div class="pub-meta">Lingyi He, 丁拼搏, Bangrang Di, Feng Zhang</div>
      <a href="#" class="badge-doi">DOI / Experimental Data</a>
    </div>
    <div class="pub-figure"><img src="/publication/2025-jge-torsional-wave/featured.png"></div>
  </div>

  <div class="pub-entry">
    <div class="pub-main">
      <h3 class="pub-title-link">利用人造岩石样品开展地震岩石物理实验研究的新进展</h3>
      <p class="pub-abstract">综述了实验室在人造岩石样品制备工艺上的创新，阐述了其在模拟复杂油气储层微观结构方面的独特优势，为地震波传播机制研究奠定了样件基础。</p>
